About Xplorer

Xplorer represent a community of people who likes to travel and explore the world. Our mission is to provide a platform where people can share their travel experiences.

We are focused on creating or renewing memories in the places people have visited or would love to visit someday. Our hope is that people can feel connected to places and people they would love to visit and experience the richness in diversity.
