Overview

Xplorer

The name Xplorer represent a community of people who like to travel and explore the world.

Site Purpose

Xplorer is focused on creating or renewing memories in the places people have visited or would love to visit someday.
Information about the places and other important facts are listed.

Scenarios

  • 1. What are some significant locations in France?
  • 2. Where can I go this summer vacation?
  • 3. Would Spain be an ideal location to create a holiday special with my family?
